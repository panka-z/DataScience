The Problem at hand:


Assume, I am student or a programmer or a business analyst trying to solve a problem at hand. Presently I am doing the task at hand in a non-automated way. The tools I use have some paid options, which is company isn't interested to buy. I am looking out for some solutions on Google, I stumble on following hot / Ad links on Google :

  1. Udemy offering some courses upto $90. Most of courses don't solve open realtime problems.
  2. Cousera / Udacity ask for monthly membership and are too long to remain dedicated online.
  3. Edureka or Great Lakes or UpGrad are way too expensive. Price ranging from 30k to 4 Lacs for their online courses.
  4. Some stack overflow answers where I could solve my problem partially, But I didn't understand what / how it happened. Hence I am not able to reproduce it.
  5. Class room coaching under a trainer is the only best option, but that may not be feasible in terms of time or reach-ability or both.

Don't worry, our team is here to solve this problem


Well, we are coming up very aggressively with set of Micro courses each explaining a concept in detail , solving lots of simple real time problems and learning through running codes in Jupyter Notebooks. Each Micro-course would be between 10 to 12 hrs with 75% reading + executing codes and 25% via video content(in hindi and english mix).

Everyone faces problems and get doubts. Hence the authors of the micro-course themselves take responsibility of solving your doubts either in the form of text or video answers on whats-app group. So, you get best quality content, lots of coded examples and doubt solving facility, and more... for less than $44 (or INR 3000) for each micro-course.


Assessment & Certifications:


Each micro-course has a case study on kaggle to be solved. The same would be assessed by the Author of the course through a telephonic interview or through a video call. Here the author/mentor would like to the understand your process of solving, your approach to the problem and the major steps you would use in coding. After a successful telephonic interview and your kaggle-colab work assessment only the author/mentor would award a e-Certificate of Completion.

Each micro-course has a deadline of one month for completion. Trust us, you can easily do it, once you start it. We the authors promise to be your best teachers while you learn ML from the core Concepts to Problem solving.
